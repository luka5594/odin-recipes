Arancini

Description

The most typical sicilian food, it consist in a cone of rice with inside several different sauce like ragù or besciamella, when is ready it has to be breaded and fried

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Brodo
  • Onion
  • Ragù
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Cook the rice with 2,5 L for KG of rice
  2. Make the ragù or whatever you want to put inside
  3. Insert the stuffed in the rice giving it a cone form
  4. Prepare pastella with sparkling water and rice flour
  5. Weat the cone with pastella and then pass it in bread crumbs
  6. Fry the cone and then enjoy it!!!!
